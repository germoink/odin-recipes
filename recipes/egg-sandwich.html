Egg Sandwich Recipe

Egg Sandwich with Egg pancakes

An egg sandwich is a sandwich with a cooked egg filling. Fried eggs, scrambled eggs, omelette, sliced boiled eggs and egg salad are great options.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bread

How to step-by-step

  1. Beat eggs, milk, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Cook eggs in hot skillet for about 1 1/2 minutes without stirring
  3. Flip eggs and cook without stirring for about 1 minute. Remove from heat
  4. Spread mayonnaise on each piece of bread, you can toast the bread if you like before applying mayonnaise
  5. Place eggs on one piece of bread/toast and top with the remaining toast
