Lasagna Recipe

Triple cheese meat lasagna

Lasagna is a baked italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese and tomato sauce. This recipe will be a mixture of all of the above. Has been passed down in my family for 4 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Basil leaves
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozarella, and Ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Step-By-Step How To

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    5. Layering the Lasagna
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with Parmesan

  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
